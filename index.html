Overview

Yeeps Hide & Seek is a playful, fast‑paced mini‑game where players compete in a colorful world of seekers and hiders. Built with vibrant visuals and bouncy energy inspired by Yeeps.com, the game focuses on quick movement, tight hiding spots, and fun interactions.

Gameplay

During a game, there are two roles. One is a Hider. As a Hider, you need to run, hide, dodge, and win. The other is a Seeker. As a Seeker, you need to find and tag all the Hiders before time runs out.

  • Hiders — Find spots, blend in, and avoid getting tagged.
  • Seekers — Track down all hiders before the timer ends.

Movement is like Gorilla Tag locomotion, as for it is using Gorilla Tag's locomotion.

Features

  • Iconic chunky font based on Londrina Solid.
  • Dynamic hiding props and environmental tricks.
  • Short 5 minute rounds to keep gameplay lively.
  • Customizable avatars.
